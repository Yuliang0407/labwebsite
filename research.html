Our Research

Our lab is dedicated to exploring the intricate communications between dietary components and the brain, with a focus on understanding their impact on human health. We aim to uncover how these interactions influence energy metabolism and behavioral outcomes, and how they can be leveraged to address chronic diseases such as obesity, diabetes, insomnia, and depression.

Through our research, we strive to contribute valuable insights that can aid in the prevention and treatment of these widespread health challenges. Our ultimate goal is to improve the quality of life through science-based dietary interventions and to provide tools for the healthcare community to combat chronic illnesses.

Our cutting-edge techniques allow us to dive deep into the biochemical processes that link nutrition and brain function. By bridging the gap between diet and neurological health, we aim to unlock new therapeutic approaches and contribute to better healthcare outcomes.
